# 🤝 Contributing to OS2sandbox/connector-poc

This repository is a collaborative sandbox for building and sharing public sector integration prototypes via hackathons and potential individual contributions.

We welcome all contributions in the form of code, config templates, documentation, and ideas.

---
<br>

## 🧩 Current Branching Strategy 2025

A lightweight branching model to support parallel work and make it easy to participate.

- `main`: Stable, production-ready code.
- `hackathon-fall-2025-base`: Shared branch for participants in the Fall Hackathon 2025.
- `feature/<team>/<short-description>`: Individual contribution branches.

<br>

## :recycle: Hackathon contribution workflow

1. **Fork the repository** and clone your fork.
2. **Create a feature branch** from `hackathon-fall-2025-base`:
3. **Make your changes**
    > code, templates, configurations, docs.
4. **Commit with clear messages** 
    > Please use [Conventional Commits](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/en/v1.0.0/) to help keep history readable and automation-friendly.
5. **Keep your branch in sync** with hackathon-base to avoid conflicts.  
    > Resolve any conflicts before pushing again.
6. **Push** your branch to GitHub
7. **Open a Pull Request** into `hackathon-fall-2025-base`.

<br>

## :bulb: Have an idea to contribute?

- **Create an issue** in the [issuetracker](https://github.com/OS2sandbox/connector-poc/issues) to share your idea, suggestion, or proposal.
